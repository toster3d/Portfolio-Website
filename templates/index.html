Professional Background

My journey began in the legal world, where I thrived and developed my skills successfully. However, my true calling for adventure led me to embark on a life-changing decision. I packed my bags, moved to England, and diligently saved up for a dream journey to Southeast Asia. It was during this expedition that I realized my innate need for freedom and creativity couldn't be fulfilled by my current profession. Determined to pursue a more fulfilling path, I transitioned to the world of programming. My learning journey kicked off with the renowned CS50 course at Harvard University Online, where I fell in love with Python. I completed the course in June 2023, marking the beginning of my coding adventure. Driven by curiosity, I delved into Git and Github through Udemy, constantly expanding my knowledge in Python, web design, and Data Science. I thrive on challenges and strive for continuous improvement, learning, and skill development. My past experiences, including teamwork, individual tasks, and managing repetitive assignments, contribute to my toolkit as a developer. I'm focused on priorities, adaptable, and ready to make a positive impact in the coding world.
